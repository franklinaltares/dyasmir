Batangas State University - TNEU Survey Respondent Agreement

You are invited to participate in a research project about leadership and recruitment for gender diversity in the construction industry. This online survey should take about 10 to 15 minutes to complete. Participation is voluntary, and responses will be kept anonymous to the degree permitted by the technology being used. You have the option to not respond to any affirm that you are at least 18 years of age. There are no direct benefits, risks, or compensation to you for participating in the study. All study data will be collected through an online survey-collection program called Quatrics. Quatrics is a secure site with SAS 70 certification for regorous privacy standard. Any data that you provided through this program will be encrypted for security purposes using Secure Socket Layers (SSL). Only the study investigators will have access to the databon Quatrics. To protect your privacy, all participants'IP address will be marked by Quatrics and will be unavailable to, and unidentifiable by, investigators or other. Quatrics' privacy policy can be obtained at http:www.qualtrics.com/privacy-statement. If you have any questions about the research, please contact the Principal Investigator, Anne Morello, Master's student in the Department of Construction Management, via email at "morelloanne.ufl.edu" or the Faculty Advisor, Dr. Raymond Issa at "raymond-issa.ufl.edu". For information for you rights as a research participant, contact the IRB at 352-392-0433.
